Mercury Tours Home Page
    Welcome to Mercury Tours where blue skies, green grass and champagne are awaiting you.
    With over 75 years combined experience in the travel and tourism industry our staff has the ability to provide you with the most complete tours or transportation available. Our equipment and drivers are S.P.A.B. Certified (School Pupil Activity Busses) and our drivers are also lecture certified. 
    Family owned and operated since 1982, we are dedicated to giving you the best experience possible while we chauffeur you around in absolute luxury. Our new and late model equipment can seat 23 to 55 passengers and most have VCR/DVD and Stereo capabilities. All our equipment is air conditioned and are equipped with a public address system so all riders can hear the information their host is providing. Our long Range buses have restrooms for your convenience.

    We provide group travel, airport arival and departures pickup and drop off, tours of San Francisco and specialized tours.
